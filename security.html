Data Security

At AuraMax, we implement security measures comparable to modern banking and SaaS platforms. Your trust is our asset.

Encryption

We use end-to-end encryption (TLS 1.3) during all data transfers to ensure your uploads cannot be intercepted.

Auto-Deletion

Analysis images are processed in ephemeral containers and automatically deleted. We do not build databases of user faces.

Secure Cloud

Our infrastructure is hosted on enterprise-grade cloud providers with limited internal access controls.

Payment Safety

We do not store credit card numbers. All payments are handled by Stripe/Chargily, complying with PCI-DSS Level 1 standards.

Breach Protocol

In the unlikely event of a security issue, we are committed to:

  • Investigating immediately upon detection.
  • Containing the issue to prevent data loss.
  • Notifying affected users transparently if required by law.
