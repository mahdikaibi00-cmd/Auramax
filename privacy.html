Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

At AuraMax, your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered personal presentation analysis platform.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Photos you upload for AI analysis
  • Account details (email, name if provided)
  • Support messages or inquiries

b) Automatically Collected Data

  • Device type and browser
  • General location (country-level)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used)

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as government IDs, financial details (handled securely by Stripe/Chargily), or biometric identifiers for ID purposes.

2. How Your Photos Are Handled

Your uploaded images are used solely to generate your personalized analysis report.

  • ✔ Processed securely via encryption
  • ✔ Not shared with other users
  • ✔ Not used for advertising
  • ✔ Not publicly displayed
  • ✔ Automatically deleted after processing or within a short retention window

We do not operate a rating or public comparison system.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver AI-based analysis and reports
  • Improve platform performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure system security and fraud prevention

We never sell personal data.

4. Data Sharing

We only share data with secure hosting providers, analytics services (non-identifying data), and payment processors (for transactions only). All partners are required to follow strict data protection standards.

5. Your Rights

You may request data deletion, request account removal, or withdraw consent at any time. Contact: support@auramax.ai

6. Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and restricted access controls to protect your data.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the platform means you accept updates.
